The Plays

Not Features. But What Moves To Make.

1. Fan Heat Map

Shows where real fans live and where momentum is rising.

Used for: Tour routing · Pop-ups · City merch · Geo-targeted ads

Houston, Atlanta, Birmingham convert 3x better than LA or NY. You route shows there first, run local ads only in those cities. Fewer shows, more profit.

2. Momentum Alerts

Flags songs, clips, or moments before they peak.

Used for: Shifting ad spend · Follow-up content · Playlist pitches · Sync timing

Outcome: Ride moments instead of missing them.

3. TikTok Sound Advocates

Shows who's already pushing your sound. Not influencers you guess at—creators getting results.

Used for: Release seeding · Coordinated posting · Spark ads with real content

You're dropping a new song. The dashboard gives you 12 creators already using your catalog. You send them the song early, coordinate a 48-hour window. Release launches with built-in momentum.

4. YouTube Catalog Revival

Turns YouTube into steady monthly income.

Used for: Adding mid-rolls · Reviving catalog · Shorts that feed long-form

Older videos still get views but low revenue. Add mid-rolls at exact timestamps, update titles, cut 5 Shorts from the same video. Old content pays again without filming anything new.

5. Facebook Fan Monetization

Shows what fans actually pay for.

Used for: Stars content · Fan subscriptions · Story-based posts that convert

Outcome: Facebook becomes a revenue channel, not a time sink.

6. Ad Spend Direction

Shows where fans are cheapest and highest quality.

Used for: Cutting waste · Doubling down on winning cities · Fewer, better ads

Outcome: Same budget, better results.

7. Brand & Partnership Finder

Matches fan behavior to brands, podcasts, and creators.

Used for: Brand pitches · Creator partnerships · Sync targeting

Outcome: Brand money that actually fits.
